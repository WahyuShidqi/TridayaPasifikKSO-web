Facility & Utility

Our Facility

Where The Quality Begins

We take pride in our advanced facility, designed to bring you the freshest, highest quality seafood with top-notch safety and efficiency. Here’s what makes us stand out:

  • Cleanliness: Our laundry room keeps all equipment and uniforms in perfect condition, ensuring a hygienic work environment.
  • Strict Quality Control:Our on-site lab tests raw materials to make sure everything meets the highest safety and quality standards.
  • Careful Selection: Every fresh catch is thoroughly inspected in our receiving area before it moves forward for processing.
  • Expert Processing: Our skilled team turns top-quality seafood into premium products, following strict food safety protocols.
  • Fast & Secure Shipping: Our optimized loading area ensures quick and safe deliveries, so your seafood arrives fresh and on time.

From the moment seafood arrives to the moment it reaches you, we handle everything with care and precision. That’s our factory's goal
