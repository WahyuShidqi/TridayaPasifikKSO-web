Welcome to Tridaya Pasifik KSO

We offer you quality and fresh fish product

We offer you premium quality, fresh fish products that are carefully sourced and handled with the utmost care. Our commitment to freshness ensures that every product meets the highest standards, providing you with a taste that’s both delicious and fresh

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Enhancing Sustainable Fisheries Production: Develop and implement sustainable fishing practices that prioritize marine resource conservation and ecosystem preservation to meet long-term market demands
  • Innovating in Fisheries Technology: Adopt the latest technology in aquaculture, processing, and distribution to boost efficiency, improve product quality, and enhance competitiveness
  • Empowering Coastal Communities: Support fishermen and fish farmers through training, mentoring, and improved market access to enhance their livelihoods and create job opportunities
  • Ensuring Product Quality and Safety: Produce high-quality fishery products that meet strict food safety standards, with a strong focus on cleanliness and halal compliance
  • Promoting Education and Environmental Awareness: Raise public awareness about the importance of preserving marine resources and encourage responsible consumption to ensure the sustainability of fisheries

Becoming a leading company that contributes to global food security through sustainable, high-quality, and environmentally friendly fish production while improving the welfare of coastal communities
