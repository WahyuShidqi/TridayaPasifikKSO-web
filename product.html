TUNA PRODUCT

Our tuna products are sourced from trusted fisheries and handled with care throughout every stage of processing to ensure optimal freshness, texture, and safety. Naturally rich in protein, omega-3 fatty acids, and essential nutrients, our tuna delivers both health benefits and excellent taste. With its firm texture and mild flavor, it is suitable for a variety of culinary uses, including grilling, searing, canning, or serving raw in dishes like sashimi and poke. Each cut is prepared to meet the expectations of both home cooks and food professionals seeking consistency and quality in every portion.

SKIPJACK TUNA PRODUCTS

Skipjack tuna, commonly called cakalang, is a smaller, fast-swimming species known for its distinctive dark stripes and rich flavor. It has a firm, oily flesh that is darker than other tuna varieties, making it well-suited for smoking and preservation. This species is prized for its bold taste and is often used in canned tuna products as well as fresh preparations. High in protein and healthy fats, skipjack offers many benefits for consumption.

OCTOPUS PRODUCTS

Octopus features a tender yet firm texture with a mild, slightly sweet flavor that adapts well to various cooking methods. Known for its rich source of protein, vitamins, and minerals, octopus is prized in many cuisines for both its nutritional value and versatility. It can be prepared grilled, boiled, or used in salads and stews, maintaining a delicate balance of flavor and chewiness. Carefully handled to maintain freshness and quality, ensuring suitability for a wide range of recipes and cooking styles.

SPANISH MACKERELS PRODUCTS

Tenggiri, also known as Spanish mackerels, is a fast-swimming fish valued for its firm, white flesh and delicate, mild flavor. Its texture is lean yet tender, making it ideal for grilling, frying, or steaming. Rich in protein and low in fat, tenggiri is a versatile choice for a variety of dishes, offering a clean taste that pairs well with many seasonings and sauces. Handled carefully to preserve its natural qualities, making it a reliable ingredient in many dishes.
